ABOUT US

STAR (Student Team for Astro-Research) is a team of students of IIT Kharagpur who are interested in space technology and its applications

OUR MISSON

The mission of this center is to design, fabricate, test, and operate nano-satellites, i.e., satellites in the 1 kg to 50kg in weight

The project’s vision is to inculcate the spirit of science and technology in the minds of young scientists and engineers, and unleash the potential that rests in them. It hopes to inspire them to have the confidence to take up challenging tasks and work towards it collectively.

WE LOVE WHAT WE DO


Mission

The first Satellite 'Vikram' will be India's first satellite based Space Observatory. Its unique payload will carryout variablity survey of M81 or Virgo super-cluster.

This project is being started as an avenue for interseted student to gain experience with space technologies


Work Flow

The project timeline is divided into five phases. Currently, we are in the first phase with the conceptual design review of the satellite. Work on the development model of the payload and the other sub-systems have begun, marking the beginning of the first phase.


consortium

Cubesat consortium with IIT Kharagpur in terms of infrastructure, technical advice, mentorship, and administrative support.
